### project name: doordielike

the name doordielike is in reference to DO or DIE by xikers. that was my first
inspiration when i started the beat.

menace was looking to collab for a miku expo submission 2-3 weeks before the deadline,
and i had a beat + part of a verse melody that i had been cooking since march,
thinking this would be a cool song for miku expo. the beat itself had an infectious
energy that i thought would be amazing live.

menace came up with the theme and most of the melody/lyrics in like 3 days. crazy
